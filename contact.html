Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official council website?

No. This is an independent community information website created by people who love Newark-on-Trent. For official council services and information, please visit the Newark & Sherwood District Council website.

How do I get to Newark-on-Trent?

Newark is easily accessible by train (London King's Cross to Newark North Gate in ~1h 20m) or by car via the A1 or A46. See our Visitor Information page for full travel details.

When is Newark market?

Newark market runs on Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday on the Market Square. Saturday is typically the biggest market day. Check our Events page for more details.

Where can I find out about upcoming events?

Our Events page lists the major annual events and regular happenings in Newark. For the most up-to-date listings, we recommend checking the Newark & Sherwood District Council website and local social media groups.

I've spotted an error on the website. How can I let you know?

We appreciate corrections! Please use the contact form above and select "Correction or Update" as the subject. We'll review and update the information as soon as possible.

Can I suggest an event or business to be added?

We welcome suggestions. Use the contact form above and select "Suggestion" as the subject, or "Submit an Event" if you'd like an event listed. Please include as much detail as possible.
