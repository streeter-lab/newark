What's On

From world-famous antiques fairs to community festivals, there's always something happening in Newark-on-Trent.

Featured Event

Multiple dates throughout the year

Newark International Antiques & Collectors Fair

One of the largest and most prestigious antiques events in Europe. Held at Newark Showground, this extraordinary fair brings together over 2,000 dealers from across the continent, offering everything from fine furniture and jewellery to vintage fashion, ceramics, prints, and curious oddities.

Whether you're a serious collector or simply love browsing, the Newark Antiques Fair is an experience not to be missed. Early bird entry is available for the keenest treasure hunters.

Annual Events & Highlights

Newark's calendar is packed with events that bring the community together and attract visitors from far and wide.

Summer

Newark Festival

A week-long celebration of music, arts, and community. Live performances, street food, family activities, and cultural events take over the town centre each June.

June
Summer

Civil War Re-enactments

Experience history brought to life at Newark Castle with dramatic Civil War re-enactments. Musket fire, cavalry charges, and living history camps transport you back to the 1640s.

Various dates
Winter

Newark Christmas Market

The Market Square transforms into a festive wonderland with artisan stalls, seasonal treats, carol singing, and a magical atmosphere for all the family.

December
Monthly

Newark Farmers Market

Local producers sell fresh meat, cheese, bread, vegetables, preserves, and artisan goods directly to customers. A great way to taste the best of Nottinghamshire's produce.

Monthly
Year-round

Palace Theatre Shows

Newark's Palace Theatre hosts a diverse programme of drama, comedy, music, children's shows, and the beloved annual pantomime. Check their website for the latest listings.

Throughout the year
Seasonal

Castle & Heritage Events

Throughout the year, Newark Castle hosts a variety of events including outdoor cinema, heritage open days, guided tours, archaeology events, and seasonal celebrations.
