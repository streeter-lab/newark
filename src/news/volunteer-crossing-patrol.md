Nottinghamshire County Council is appealing for a volunteer school crossing patrol officer at the Balderton Gate junction near The Magdalene School. The position involves helping children cross safely during morning and afternoon school runs.

Full training and equipment are provided. Anyone interested should contact the council's road safety team or speak to the school office.
